# EnterpriseVerifi: Closed-Loop Verification Platform
## Investor Brief

---

### **1. Executive Summary**

**EnterpriseVerifi** is a closed-loop verification system for global supply chains. We provide businesses with a single, unified platform to verify shipping, insurance, and quality control, replacing fragmented, manual, and fraud-prone processes.

By leveraging real-time data integrations and providing immutable audit trails, EnterpriseVerifi drastically reduces operational costs, mitigates supply chain risks, and unlocks new efficiencies. Our target customers are enterprises in logistics, manufacturing, and high-value goods, who currently spend millions on manual verification and dispute resolution.

**The platform is not a concept; it is a working MVP focused on delivering immediate ROI by solving critical business verification challenges.**

---

### **2. The Problem: The High Cost of Mistrust**

Global supply chains run on a complex web of paper documents, siloed systems, and manual checks. This creates massive inefficiencies and risks:

*   **High Verification Costs**: Businesses spend up to 15% of a shipment's value on manual document verification, inspections, and insurance checks.
*   **Fraud & Disputes**: Forged documents, counterfeit goods, and quality disputes cost global businesses billions annually.
*   **Lack of Visibility**: Poor visibility into shipment status, quality, and compliance leads to costly delays and broken supply chains.

---

### **3. The Solution: The EnterpriseVerifi Platform**

EnterpriseVerifi is an all-in-one platform built on three core modules:

*   **1. Shipping & Logistics Verification**: Real-time verification of shipping milestones (FOB, CIF), customs documentation, and live shipment tracking through API and IoT integrations.
*   **2. Insurance & Finance Integration**: Automated validation of cargo and title insurance policies, streamlined claims processing, and a foundation for supply chain financing against verified assets.
*   **3. Quality & Compliance Verification**: Digital verification of quality certifications (e.g., GIA, organic), inspection reports, and regulatory compliance, ensuring assets meet required standards.

---

### **4. Market Opportunity & Business Model**

The market for supply chain verification and traceability is projected to grow to over $30 billion by 2028.

*   **Target Market**: Enterprises in logistics, luxury goods, commodities, and manufacturing with complex global supply chains.
*   **Business Model**: A tiered SaaS subscription model based on verification volume and the number of integrated modules. Pricing ranges from **$10k to $50k+ per month** per enterprise client.
*   **Projected ARR**: With a clear path to market, we project a **$100M+ ARR** opportunity by capturing a small fraction of this underserved market.
